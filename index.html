Our Story

More Than Just Chicken

Fat Roosters in Hemel Hempstead is where flame-grilled Peri Peri meets crispy fried perfection. From our signature grilled collection to fresh smash burgers, doner kebabs, loaded boxes and indulgent milkshakes — we've built a full menu for every craving.

Every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and real care. Our Peri Peri chicken is flame-grilled daily in your choice of heat — from Lemon & Herb all the way to Extra Hot 🔥. No shortcuts. Just proper food, done right.
